Services we offer.

ILETS

The International English Language Test (IELTS) is designed to help you work, study or migrate to a country where English is the native language. Your ability to listen, read, write and speak in English will be assessed during the test.

OET

OET is a international English language test for the healthcare sector. Established in the late 1980s under contract to the Australian Federal Government. Since 2013 OET has been owned by Cambridge Boxhill Language Assessment.
